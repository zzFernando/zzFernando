## About Me

I am an engineer and M.Sc. student in **Artificial Intelligence** at the **Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul (UFRGS)**.  
My background spans **software engineering, cloud architecture, aeronautical sciences, and machine learning**.

My research focuses on **neuroevolution**, **population-based learning**, and the **analysis and visualization of learning dynamics in neural network weight spaces**, with strong emphasis on **interpretable, reliable, and reproducible AI systems**.

In parallel, I work as a **Cloud Architect and AI/ML Engineer**, designing and operating **secure, scalable, and cost-efficient cloud-native systems**, bridging **AI research and production-grade infrastructure**.
